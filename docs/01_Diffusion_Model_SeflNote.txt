# What is DM?

	* A Diffusion Model is a type of Generative AI that creates High Quality data primarily Image but also Audio & Video by learning how to reverse a gradual(progressive) 'diffusion' process.


	* A diffusion model learns how data (images) look by learning how to reverse a noise process step by step.


	* It has 2 main steps:

		1. Forward Diffusion/Process or Noising (Uses known physics, easy part)
		2. Backward Diffusion/Process or Denoising (Uses probability, hard and research worthy)


	* DDPM -> Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Model, a paper published in 2020 that provided the mathematical framework of noise removing process.




# What is Gaussian (Normal) Distribution?

	* The Gaussian distribution is a probability distribution that looks like a symmetric, bell-shaped curve.
	
	* It has 2 key parameters

		1. Mean: The center of the bell. This is the "average" value.
		2. Variance or Standard Deviation: This determines how "spread out" or "flat" the bell is.

	

# Important Note:

	* High Variance or Standard Deviation: High Uncertainty and flat bell
	* Low Variance or Standard Deviation: High certainty
